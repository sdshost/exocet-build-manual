Wiring Front lights

Contents / Wiring Front lights
  1. Fit headlights to mounting brackets with 2 thick washers above the bracket.
  2. The indicator tube should be mounted below with the nut inside.
  3. Fit the bulb holder/lens/wires in the indicator tube, grey/red wire to center pin.
  4. Thread the wires out of a lower inner hole and join with the headlights.
  5. Using a 6 way connector is recomended encase you need to remove the lights, or a 5way and 2 way if you want each part seperate.
  6. Use the table below to work out what wires go where. This was our model, confirm before finalising joins.
